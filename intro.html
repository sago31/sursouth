The South now understood as a place to be dreamt about but struggling because of its past and present. There’s a force pulling students from the Global South to come to the North as a prescribed path to success. I’ve set out to listen to these unique voices. Their ideas on what they thought they would find in the north, against what they’ve found. What they left behind, and what they’re longing for. In the end, they might still be looking what they were trying to find, they might think they have found it, or they could have gone lost in their journey.
